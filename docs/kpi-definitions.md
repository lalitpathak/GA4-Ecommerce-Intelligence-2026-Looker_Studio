# 📊 KPI Definitions – Digital Marketing Analytics Dashboard

This document defines all key performance indicators (KPIs) used in the Digital Marketing Analytics Dashboard.

---

# 🌐 Traffic KPIs

## Sessions
The total number of visits to the website within a given time period. A session may include multiple page views and interactions.

## Users (New Users)
The number of unique visitors who accessed the website for the first time.

## Page Views
Total number of pages viewed. Repeated views of a single page are also counted.

---

# 📈 Engagement KPIs

## Engagement Rate
Percentage of sessions that were engaged (e.g., lasted longer than 10 seconds, included conversions, or had multiple page views).

## Bounce Rate (if applicable)
Percentage of sessions where users left after viewing only one page.

## Average Session Duration
The average time users spend on the website per session.

---

# 💰 Revenue KPIs

## Total Revenue
The total monetary value generated from conversions or purchases.

## Revenue per User (RPU)
Revenue divided by total users.

## Conversion Value
Total value generated from completed conversions.

# 🌍 Geographic KPIs

## Top Locations
Countries/cities generating the highest traffic or revenue.

# 📱 Device & Platform KPIs

## Device Category
Breakdown of users by:
- Desktop
- Mobile
- Tablet

## Browser Share
Distribution of users across browsers (Chrome, Safari, etc.)

# 🧠 Notes

These KPIs are aligned with Google Analytics 4 (GA4) reporting standards and are commonly used in digital marketing dashboards.
